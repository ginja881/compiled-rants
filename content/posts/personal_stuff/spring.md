Hey readers!! Hope you are all doing well and living life to the fullest wherever you are!!

Y'know, one question that always feels like "what is your favorite color?" is "what is your favorite season?"
I mean, it's not only an option with four very distinct answers (aliens probably have more answers to this question) that 
reveals a bit about someone's personality, character, or just sheer bias. For example, literally some of my buddies
love winter for the festivities and activities, such as Christmas and skiing. However, others view it as an annoying drag. 

What would be your favorite season?

Honestly, from living in North America/typically cold places in the world, Spring and Summer are my favorite seasons because

1. Sunny!! Who doesn't like some good old sun and vitamin C?
2. Objectively more fun activities are practical, such as rowing, hiking, and biking.
3. Depending on where you live, the vibe is better and isn't depressing like Winter sometimes.
Literally the number one reason why I hate North Dakota as a state is what it is like during Winter.


However, from living in Arizona for a few years, it can suck sometimes. I'd never want to live in a place to where trash cans 
and anything plastic melts just because of weather. Rather have some form of balance between heat and cold.

Anyways, hope you all have an amazing Spring and Summer!!!
